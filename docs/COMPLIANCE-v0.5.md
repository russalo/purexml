## §6 Measure-first
Re-confirmed the attack-class → mitigation-layer mapping (most established in v0.1.2 +
`/deep-research`). On the audit runtime (expat 2.6.1): billion-laughs / quadratic /
external-entity-XXE / external-DTD-retrieval = **BLOCKED** by purexml's default-on
handlers (version-independent — earlier and stronger than libexpat's cap); large-tokens
(CVE-2023-52425) = **EXPAT_MITIGATED** (≥2.6.0); disproportionate-memory = **LIVE**
(below the 2.7.2 floor); structural-DoS = **OPT_IN**. The report errs toward
*under-stating* protection at every version boundary (fail-safe).
